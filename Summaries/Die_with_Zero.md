## Short AI Summary  
Bill Perkins’ *Die with Zero* argues that true wealth is measured by the richness of experiences, not by the size of a bank balance. He proposes a “memory‑dividend” model where early, well‑timed experiences compound emotional satisfaction over a lifetime. The book blends practical financial tools—like insurance and annuities—with a life‑planning framework that balances health, wealth, and time to maximize fulfillment. Perkins urges readers to spend deliberately, give strategically, and live fully before the inevitable end.
